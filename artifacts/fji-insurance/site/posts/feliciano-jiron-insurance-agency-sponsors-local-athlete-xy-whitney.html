Feliciano Jiron Insurance Agency Sponsors Local Athlete Xy Whitney

At Feliciano Jiron Insurance Agency, community support goes beyond policies. It’s about investing in people, dreams, and the spirit of Las Vegas. That’s why we’re proud to sponsor Xy Whitney, a dedicated athlete and inspiring local figure who is making waves both on and off the field.

Xy Whitney is not only a member of the Las Vegas Silver Stars, but also a student, a mom, and a full-contact football player. In addition to all of this, she is a board-certified health coach working full time, further showcasing her commitment to helping others live healthier, stronger lives.

Her journey is a powerful example of determination, resilience, and balance. All qualities that resonate deeply with our values as a local business.

After signing with the Silver Stars in early January, Xy officially began her rookie season, stepping into a challenging and competitive environment with confidence and drive. Full-contact women’s football demands strength, discipline, and commitment, and Xy embodies all three. Whether she’s on the field pushing her limits, coaching clients toward better health, in the classroom working toward her goals, or at home caring for her family, she continues to show what it truly means to pursue your passions relentlessly.

At Feliciano Jiron Insurance Agency, we believe in uplifting individuals who make a difference in our community. Sponsoring Xy is more than just supporting an athlete, it’s about recognizing a role model who inspires others to chase their dreams, no matter how demanding life may be.

We are honored to be part of Xy Whitney’s journey and look forward to cheering her on throughout her rookie season and beyond. Her story is a reminder that strength comes in many forms, and here in Las Vegas, it’s stronger than ever.
